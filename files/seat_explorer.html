Conditional Seat Scenario Explorer

Upload a posterior draws CSV for party seat counts. The app calculates the unconditional medians and component-wise credible intervals directly from those draws. Use the scenario controls to ask a question like “What if Liberal got at least 170 seats?” The app then filters the retained integer seat allocations and recomputes the conditional summaries from those simulations.

Inputs and controls

The draws file should have one row per posterior draw and one column per party. Draw values are treated as integer seat counts, and the app computes medians and intervals directly from the draws.
